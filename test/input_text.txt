In a small town nestled between rolling hills and dense forests, the villagers spoke of a mysterious library that appeared only under the light of a full moon. Legends told of books that contained not words, but memories, capable of revealing secrets long forgotten. Curious travelers often ventured into the woods, hoping to glimpse the ethereal structure, yet most returned with nothing but the whisper of the wind and the rustle of leaves. Among the villagers, young Elena felt a strange pull toward the unknown, as if the stories themselves were guiding her steps.
